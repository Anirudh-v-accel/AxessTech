The Placement Assistance Program is a specialized course designed to provide in-depth training in Software Testing or Front-End Development. It includes hands-on experience, real-time projects, and placement assistance upon completion.

Our placement assistance means that after successfully completing our training and projects, we will help you find a job. We leverage our industry connections to match candidates with top companies, helping you secure employment.

The duration of the training varies depending on the course track you choose, but typically it takes around 120 days to complete the training and project phases before moving to placement.

No prior experience is needed to join the Placement Assistance Program. Our courses are designed for both beginners and those looking to transition into Software Testing or Front-End Development roles.

You will work on real-world, industry-relevant projects that simulate actual work environments. These projects are specifically tailored to either Software Testing or Front-End Development based on the track you choose.

We have partnered with financial institutions to provide loan options that cover your placement charges. You can repay the loan in easy EMIs, making it a flexible and affordable option for everyone.

After training, we provide extensive support, including Career Counselling, Shadowing on Live Projects, and Group Discussion Session with Industry Experts until you secure a job.

At Axess Technology, we focus on hands-on, real-time projects that prepare you for actual job scenarios. Our Placement Assistance Program provides not only top-notch training but also job search support through our network of partner companies.

You can choose to specialize in either Software Testing or Front-End Development, depending on your career goals. We offer distinct tracks for both, ensuring focused and relevant training.
